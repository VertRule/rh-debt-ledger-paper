# Zero-Free Region Lemma

**Concept-Tag:** RH-R3-ZFR-LEMMA-1

## Claim

There exists a zero-free region for ζ(s) of the form σ > 1 − c/(log |t|)^(2/3)(log log |t|)^(1/3) for |t| ≥ t₀, where c and t₀ are effective constants.

## Inputs / Outputs

- **Input:** Definition of ζ(s) and its analytic continuation
- **Output:** Region R in the critical strip where ζ(s) ≠ 0

## Dependencies

- Korobov (1958)
- Vinogradov (1958)
- de la Vallée Poussin classical region (weaker alternative)
